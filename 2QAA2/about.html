Our History

Founders

Godric Gryffindor

Founder of Hogwarts, Head of House Gryffindor
  • He was said to have praised courage, determination, and strength of heart above all else.
  • He selected students with bravery to join his house.
  • Fun Fact: His hat was turned into the sorting hat in order to determine future student's houses once the founders passed.

Helga Hufflepuff

Founder of Hogwarts, Head of House Hufflepuff
  • She seeked the most loyal, hardworking, patient, and tolerant students to join her house.
  • Apart from that, she had a gift for food related charms and who's recipes continue to be the basis of many Hogwarts feasts.
  • Fun Fact: She arranged for the house-elf contingent to work at Hogwarts, providing a safe place, free of abuse.

Rowena Ravenclaw

Founder of Hogwarts, Head of House Ravenclaw
  • Her selection of students is based on intelligence and wisdom.
  • She values those traits along with creativity and cleverness.
  • Fun Fact: It is rumored that Rowena Ravenclaw personally picked the name and location of Hogwarts after dreaming of a warty hog leading her to a cliff by the lake.

Salazar Slytherin

Founder of Hogwarts, Head of House Slytherin
  • Slytherin's favored qualities included resourcefulness, determination, a certain disregard for the rules, and the ability to speak parseltongue.
  • Another quality that he searched for in his students is their blood purity.
  • Fun Fact: He constructed a secret Scriptorium. (only for those who spoke Parseltongue, had the willingness to cast a curse on somebody else)

Early History

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry was founded around 990 A.D. by four of the greatest wizards and witches of the age: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. They each represented an aspect of personality that they wanted to bring out in new students.

The first student class to graduate from the school were immortalised in a portrait that would continue to hang in the school for over 1000 years.

Middle History

The school had historically shouldered the responsibility of protecting "dangerous things" from falling into the wrong hands, and carrying the burden of the job remarkably well despite the fact that subsequent heads of the school and members of the faculty came to recognise how it compromised the security of the castle.

About three hundred years after the school was founded, the Triwizard Tournament was established as an interscholastic competition between three of the most prestigious magical schools in Europe: Hogwarts, Beauxbatons, and Durmstrang.

Administrators

  • Albus Percival Wulfric Brian Dumbledore

    Current Headmaster of Hogwarts

    Mr. Albus Dumbledore is one of the most esteemed wizards to have ever lived, holding the title of Grand Sorcerer. He has been the Headmaster of Hogwarts for 25 years and counting, while also being a Chief Warlock in charge of parliamentary affairs and court procedures.

  • Minerva McGonagall

    Current Deputy Headmistress, Gryffindor House Head, & Transfiguration Department Head

    After her education at Hogwarts, Ms. Minerva McGonagall worked at the Ministry of Magic for two years before returning, where she became the Head of Gryffindor House and the Transfiguration professor.

  • Pomona Sprout

    Current Hufflepuff House Head & Herbology Department Head

    Ms. Pomona Sprout attended Hogwarts in her youth, where she was Sorted into Hufflepuff and excelled at Herbology. She used to bring the most interesting specimens (some of which could dance and talk) from the Herbology greenhouses to decorate Hufflepuff Basement.

  • Filius Flitwick

    Current Ravenclaw House Head & Charms Master

    Mr. Filius Flitwick has a rich sense of humor and does not discriminate against the students' background. He had earned the title of Master Duelist, as he was a duelling champion during his younger years.

  • Severus Snape

    Current Slytherin House Head & Potions Master

    Aside from being a Potions Master, Mr. Severus Snape is also skilled in creating his own unique spells, one of which is the Levicorpus. He continues to practice his proficiency in magic through his teaching of Potions.

School Song

Hoggy Warty Hogwarts

Written by Headmaster Dumbledore

Hogwarts, Hogwarts, Hoggy Warty Hogwarts,
Teach us something please,
Whether we be old and bald,
Or young with scabby knees

Our heads could do with filling,
With some interesting stuff,
For now they're bare and full of air,
Dead flies and bits of fluff,

So teach us things worth knowing,
Bring back what we've forgot,
Just do your best, we'll do the rest,
And learn until our brains all rot.

Teachers

Aurora Sinistra

Professor Sinistra has been an astronomy teacher since 1985. With over 10 years of teaching experience, she is an accomplished astrologist that is strict with her students.

Filius Flitwick

Professor Flitwick is the Ravenclaw House Head, a model student who's brave enough to almost be Gryffindor, Charms Master, Choir Conductor, and in general, a powerful wizard.

Minerva McGonagall

Professor McGonagall has taught many great students during her stay in Hogwarts as a teacher. She is the Head of the Transfiguration Department and Head of Gryffindor House.

Pomona Sprout

Professor Sprout is the Head of Hufflepuff and Herbology Department in Hogwarts. She is a Hogwarts Alumna, giving back to the school as she shares her talents in Herbology.

Remus John Lupin

Professor Lupin was a Gryffindor Prefect during his high school years in Hogwarts. He is a skilled, up-and-coming professor with a passion in Defense Against the Dark Arts.

Rolanda Hooch

Professor Hooch plays a big role in the Hogwarts Quidditch scene as both a referee and coach. Alongside this, she also teaches Flying to first year students.

Rubeus Hagrid

Professor Hagrid is best known as the Keeper of Keys and Ground of Hogwarts. He assumes the post of Care of Magical Creatures professor and shows students the beauty of magical animals.

Severus Snape

The Head of Slytherin House and Potions Master Professor Snape may be considered a terror teacher by some students, but his intelligence does not go unnoticed.

Sybill Trelawney

Professor Trelawney, with the power to predict the futures of her students, teaches Divination to third years and older. She herself was the one who prophecized Harry Potter's situation.
