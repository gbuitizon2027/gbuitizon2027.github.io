Extra-Curriculars

7 extra-curricular subjects and 17 clubs, open to all witches and wizards

Subjects

  • Art & Muggle Art

    Basic muggle art and wizarding photography

  • Ghoul Studies

    Focuses on ghouls, ghosts, and poltergeists

  • Magical Theory

    For those who wish to delve deeper into the nature of magic

  • Music & Muggle Music

    Introduces spells linked to music, also examines music made by muggles

  • Xylomancy

    Includes the often overlooked method of divinaton, relying on twigs

Study Clubs

  • Dragon Club

    Founded by Margaretta Ironbark

    Accepts students with advanced skills in Defense Against the Dark Arts, Duelling, Flying and Quidditch

  • Sphinx Club

    Founded by Skylar Flame

    Accepts students with advanced skills in Astronomy, Charms, History of Magic and Transfiguration

  • Hippogriff Club

    Founded by Olin Oakwood

    Accepts students with advanced skills in Care of Magical Creatures, Divination, Herbology and Potions

Featured Club

Duelling Club

Led by Professor Severus Snape

Founded by Gilderoy Lockhart and formed due to the rising threats of dark magic, this club teaches witches and wizards how to defend themselves using specialized magic spells.

Festivities

Hallowe'en Feast

Bats and pumpkins galore! Indulge in your favorite pumpkin treats, tarts, cakes, and many different magical sweets. Keep your eyes peeled for ghosts!

Held on the 31st of October, open to all

Visits to Hogsmeade Village

Need to destress? Why not check out the High Street's specialty shops and pubs? Hogsmeade is open to visitors on the weekends, but most popular during the winter season!

For third years and above only

Christmas Feast

Enjoy the Flaming Christmas Puddings with Silver Sickles hidden in them this Christmas season! If you wish to stay during the holidays, why not celebrate with everyone else?

Held on the 25th of December, open to all
