English 3

Sonnet Writing AA

In Another Universe, I'd Be Lazing In Your Grace

These sonnets touch on the pains of yearning for something but never truly having it. What is one to do when they know they're supposed to let go but they can't, or when they're trapped in their imagination? By writing these poems, we hope to put all these feelings into words—feelings that, no matter what the reason may be, almost all individual has felt before. However, we do recognize that longing isn't just the feeling you have when you're wishing for someone. There are some who long for a new life, something different, something special. We wish that our six sonnets will be able to reach all those who are still going through their "longing" phase, and to let them know that it's perfectly normal to feel what they're feeling.
