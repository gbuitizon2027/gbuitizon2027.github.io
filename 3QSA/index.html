Game Mechanics

Welcome to our café!

Pompompurin's friends from far and wide have come to visit this cozy establishment! Choose from a selection of ingredients to fulfill every customer's order. Each order can either come in a cup, plate, or bowl. There are also three choices for the flavors, namely, avocado, strawberry, and chocolate.

Remember to mix the food up before serving it to your customer! After that step, you can't change the ingredients anymore, so make sure to choose the right items.

Sometimes, a customer might be fine with any flavor, any container, or even both!

If you give a customer the wrong order, then too bad; it's game over for the day.

The scoring is as follows:
Any container & any flavor     10pts
Any container or any flavor   15pts
Specific container & flavor     20pts

P.S. Check the console log if you want to cheat >:D
